Beef steak

"When you're hungry, go to the Grill House"

The best steaks in the world at the Grill House

Ingredients

  • 400 g beef steaks
  • 1 tbsp. l. seasonings for grilling and shish kebab “Meat on coals” KOTANYI
  • tbsp. l. olive oil
  • pcs. tomatoes

Cooking instructions

  1. Rub the meat with KOTANYI seasoning
  2. Drizzle with olive oil
  3. Grill on both sides
